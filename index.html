Nigeria is a country rich in culture, history, and diversity. Known as the Giant of Africa, it is home to over 250 ethnic groups.

Nigeria is located in West Africa and is the most populous country on the continent. It has a strong economy driven by oil, agriculture, technology, and entertainment.
